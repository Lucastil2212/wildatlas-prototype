The product

One rugged node for the place you care about

Mount a Manticore Field Node at the backyard edge, forest trail, greenhouse, or research plot. On-device AI watches, listens, and logs — without shipping your habitat to the cloud.

  • Wildlife observer Edge classification for sounds and optional camera events, with confidence and evidence clips kept local.
  • Habitat monitoring Environmental sensing plus a signed observation log you can trust later.
  • Built for the field Weatherproof enclosure, solar / battery power, Ethernet, and edge inference on a Pi-class core.

The service

Integrate when you need the network

Keep ownership of your observations. Sync peer-to-peer when you choose, or plug into Manticore data services for dashboards, aggregation, and habitat intelligence across sites.

  • Signed observation records Structured events with species, confidence, location, hashes, and model version — ready for APIs and partners.
  • PeerWeave sync Resilient gossip-style exchange between nodes and communities — offline-capable by design.
  • Optional access layer Permissions and ownership when you need controlled sharing — without making the cloud mandatory.
