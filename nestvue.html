Key features

A private window into the nest

Motion and sound detection capture visits, nesting, feeding, and birdsong — while on-device analysis keeps recordings meaningful and power use low.

  • Autonomous capture Motion- and sound-triggered recording when birds are present
  • Wide-angle HD camera Clear daytime footage with low-light and nighttime monitoring
  • Birdsong microphone High-sensitivity audio for natural sound and song recognition
  • Solar-assisted power Roof-mounted panel recharges the internal battery for long runs
  • Local encrypted storage Media stays on-device until you review it from your phone
  • Smart mobile alerts Know when a bird arrives, a nest activates, or activity looks unusual

Autonomous monitoring

Works in the background so you don’t have to

NestVue watches and listens for meaningful activity, then stores what matters — conserving battery and storage without shipping your backyard to the cloud.

  • Motion-triggered recording Records visits and nesting activity only when birds are present.
  • Day & night vision Infrared-ready monitoring for 24/7 observation in low light.
  • On-device activity analysis Edge processing identifies useful events and reduces noise — privacy-first.
  • Wireless sync Review video and audio on a connected mobile device over Wi‑Fi.

Inside the nest

Built for birds. Engineered for the field.

Bird-safe materials, a comfortable nesting chamber, and electronics kept isolated and protected — with a removable panel for seasonal cleaning.

  • Weather-resistant wood All-season enclosure designed for real outdoor mounting.
  • Safe nesting chamber Non-toxic materials with electronics sealed away from the birds.
  • Easy install & clean Mount in minutes; open the access panel for seasonal maintenance.
